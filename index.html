Going Walkabout

Joey has gone walkabout. Can you help him find his way back to his mum and sister?

How to play?

Using your arrow keys (←, ↑, →, ↓) help Joey reunite with his family.
Navigate Joey past the dingoes, use the log to get across the billabong and dodge the incoming traffic of pick up trucks.

Be careful! If Joey collides with an obstacle or falls into the billabong, he'll lose a life and be returned to the start.

Joey starts with 8 lives.
And don't forget make sure Joey gets a chance to snack along the way to earn you an extra 100points

Press “start” and help Joey find his family.
